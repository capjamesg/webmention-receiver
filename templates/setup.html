Setup Instructions ⚙️

Before you get started, please make sure your domain is configured to use IndieAuth authentication. You can do this by following the instructions on the indieauth.com Setup page. Setting up IndieAuth on your domain should only take a few minutes.

With IndieAuth set up on your home page, you are ready to log in to the webmention dashboard where you will keep track of the webmentions you receive.

After logging in, add the following tag to the <head> tag on your web page:

<link rel="webmention" href="{{ config.CLIENT_ID }}">

This tag will be used by webmention senders to find your webmention endpoint.

Once this tag has been added to your site, you are ready to start receiving webmentions on your web page.

All you have to do is to get someone to send you a webmention. That webmention should show up on your dashboard that is visible when you log in.

Please note that the webmention endpoint is only updated at the start of every hour. This means you might need to wait some time before a webmention appears on your dashboard.
